About Us

Indians have always prided themselves on having spicy food- Chilly Chicken, Chilly Paneer and Chilly everything. But do we even really know what spicy is? So that’s why with the vision of bringing Real Spicy to India, we created the JoloChip. Moreover the name Jolo comes from the Bhut Jolokia pepper, Which found’s in North East India. And it also known as Ghost Pepper it is one of the 10 hottest peppers in the World, and along with the deadly Carolina reaper. It is part of the ingredients that make up our uniquely spicy Jolochip that is guaranteed to set your insides on fire!

Made with ingredients such as corn, canola oil, edible activated charcoal powder, Carolina reaper, Trinidad Scorpion, Ghost pepper, jalapeno spice, iodized salt, calcium propionate (E282), sodium propionate (E281)
